Priya Patel

Secretary, Board of Directors

Priya Patel has served as Secretary of Rho Omega & Friends since 2020, ensuring smooth operations and clear communication across all board activities.

With a background in communications and event management, Priya coordinates our board meetings, maintains organizational records, and oversees our community outreach events. Her attention to detail and organizational skills keep our team running efficiently.

Priya holds a degree in Communications and has worked in corporate communications for over a decade. She is passionate about education access and has personally fundraised over $50,000 for our scholarship programs.

When not serving on the board, Priya enjoys photography, traveling, and cooking traditional Indian cuisine for friends and family.
