Maria Lopez

Vice Chair, Board of Directors

Maria Lopez joined the Rho Omega & Friends board in 2018 and has served as Vice Chair since 2021. Her expertise in strategic planning and community outreach has been invaluable to our organization's growth.

As a first-generation college graduate herself, Maria is deeply committed to helping students overcome financial barriers to higher education. She personally reviews scholarship applications and mentors several of our scholarship recipients.

Maria is a senior executive at a Fortune 500 company and holds an MBA from a top business school. She serves on multiple nonprofit boards and is a sought-after speaker on diversity and inclusion in education.

In her free time, Maria enjoys hiking, reading, and spending time with her family.
