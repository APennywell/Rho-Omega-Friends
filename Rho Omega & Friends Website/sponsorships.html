Sponsorship Opportunities

Sponsors make our work possible. We offer several sponsorship levels for individuals, businesses, and organizations.

Sponsorship Levels

  • Bronze Sponsor: Recognition on our website.
  • Silver Sponsor: Website recognition + event signage.
  • Gold Sponsor: Featured recognition + scholarship named in your honor.

Become a Sponsor

To become a sponsor, please contact us via the chat or email, or make a contribution on our Donate page and indicate your sponsorship level.
