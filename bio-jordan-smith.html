Jordan Smith

Treasurer, Board of Directors

Jordan Smith has served as Treasurer of Rho Omega & Friends since 2019, bringing exceptional financial expertise and fiscal responsibility to our organization.

With over 20 years of experience in accounting and financial management, Jordan ensures that every dollar donated to our organization is used efficiently and transparently to support our scholarship programs and community initiatives.

Jordan is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) and holds a degree in Finance. Prior to joining our board, Jordan worked as a partner at a major accounting firm and has extensive experience with nonprofit financial management.

Outside of work, Jordan is an avid golfer and volunteers as a financial literacy educator at local high schools.
