Alex Johnson

Chair, Board of Directors

Alex Johnson has served as Chair of Rho Omega & Friends since 2020, bringing over 15 years of leadership experience in nonprofit management and community development.

With a background in education administration and a passion for empowering underserved communities, Alex has been instrumental in expanding our scholarship programs and building lasting partnerships with local businesses and educational institutions.

Alex holds a Master's degree in Public Administration and has received numerous awards for community service, including the Regional Philanthropist of the Year award in 2019.

When not working with Rho Omega & Friends, Alex enjoys mentoring young professionals and volunteering at local youth centers.
