Scholarship Opportunities

We offer several scholarships each year for high school and college students. Review the details below and note the deadlines on our calendar.

Available Scholarships

  • Future Leaders Scholarship: For students demonstrating leadership and community service.
  • STEM Excellence Award: For students pursuing science, technology, engineering, or math.
  • Community Impact Grant: For students leading local community projects.

How to Apply

Please prepare your transcript, a personal statement, and two letters of recommendation. Application forms can be requested via our chat or email.
